# The Cure
## Storyline

-- Setting -
- Modern times
- City (Boston, NY, Etc.)

-- Plot -
- Creatures are sorted into BAD or GOOD categories, and main character can choose who to side with.

- People around the main character become infected (the failed experiment [main character] is infectious to those around him) and has to make hard decisions based on urgency, morality, etc.

-- Characters -
- The Experiment [Main character; Name and Gender chosen by player]
- The family (Wife/Husband, 3 kids [Boy 12, Girl 5, Newborn girl])
- The friends (The best friend Kyle, Kyle's sister Janice)
- The younger sibling Jakob
- The Hybrids (Medusa, Tritan, Posiden,)
- Dr. Samovly (Mad russian scientist)
